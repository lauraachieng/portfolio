Pilau

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g beef or chicken, cubed
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cups beef broth or water
  • 1 tbsp pilau masala
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the meat: In a pot, heat oil and fry onions until golden brown. Add garlic, ginger, and pilau masala, and stir for a few seconds.
  2. Cook the meat: Add the beef/chicken and let it cook until browned. Then, add tomatoes and salt, and cook until soft.
  3. Add rice: Pour in the washed rice and stir well to mix with the spices.
  4. Cook: Add beef broth/water, cover, and let it simmer on low heat for 20 minutes until the rice is fully cooked.
  5. Serve: Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot with kachumbari or banana on the side.

Nutritional information(per serving)

  • Calories: ~500 kcal
  • Protein: ~25g
  • Carbohydrates: ~80g
  • Fat: ~10g
